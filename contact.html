Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are the recipes on ChatoriCode easy to make for beginners?

A: Absolutely! ChatoriCode is designed with home cooks and beginners in mind. Many of our recipes are labeled "5-Min Quickies" or "Fast Fixes" and come with step-by-step instructions, often accompanied by visual guides (where available). We focus on simple techniques and readily available ingredients to ensure anyone can decode the crave at home. Our goal is to make street-style and fast food accessible and fun for everyone, regardless of their cooking experience. We break down complex dishes into manageable steps, providing tips and tricks to ensure success in your kitchen. If you ever get stuck, feel free to reach out via our contact form!

Q: Can I submit my own recipes to ChatoriCode?

A: We love featuring the culinary creativity of our community! Currently, we are developing a dedicated recipe submission portal. In the meantime, if you have a unique fast food, fusion snack, or street-style recipe that aligns with the ChatoriCode vibe, please send us a brief description and any accompanying photos or videos via our contact form. Our editorial team will review your submission, and if it's a good fit, we'll reach out to discuss featuring it on our platform. We're always on the lookout for bold flavors and innovative hacks from our Chatori community!

Q: How often is new content (recipes, blog posts) added to the website?

A: We're constantly cooking up new content! We aim to add new recipes weekly, with a special "Code of the Crave" feature every week decoding a popular snack. Our blog is updated regularly with new articles on snack hacks, food trends, and culinary stories. We also produce "1-Min Chatori Fix" video shorts frequently for our social media channels, which are then linked on the website. To stay updated, we highly recommend subscribing to our newsletter and following us on our social media platforms!

Q: Do you offer healthier alternatives for fast food?

A: Yes! We have a dedicated "Junk Made Healthy" category where we explore delicious ways to enjoy your favorite fast food and street snacks with a healthier twist. This includes recipes with baked or air-fried options instead of deep-fried, recipes using whole grains, and those incorporating more vegetables and leaner proteins. While our core focus is on flavor and speed, we believe in mindful indulgence and providing options for those looking to enjoy these treats with less guilt. We provide tips on ingredient substitutions and cooking methods to make your favorite dishes lighter without compromising on taste.

Q: What is the "Snack-Crate Builder" feature?

A: The "Snack-Crate Builder" is an upcoming bonus content feature designed to help you make the most of your pantry! You'll be able to input ingredients you currently have on hand, and our system will automatically suggest multiple delicious ChatoriCode recipes you can create with those ingredients. Q: How can I provide feedback or report an issue with the website?

A: Your feedback is incredibly valuable to us! The best way to provide feedback or report any technical issues you encounter on the website is by using the contact form above. Please select "Website Feedback" or "Technical Issue" as your subject, and provide as much detail as possible so our team can assist you effectively. We are committed to continuously improving your experience on ChatoriCode.

Q: Are the recipes suitable for specific dietary restrictions (e.g., vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free)?

A: While many of our recipes are naturally vegetarian due to the nature of Indian street food, we are actively working on adding specific tags and filters to help users easily find recipes that fit their dietary needs (e.g., "Vegan," "Gluten-Free options"). Currently, we recommend reviewing the ingredient list of each recipe carefully. We also provide tips for substitutions in many recipes to help you adapt them to your preferences. Our goal is to make ChatoriCode inclusive for all food lovers.
